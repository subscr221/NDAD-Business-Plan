# Nitrodynamics - Investment Committee One-Page Summary


## The Thesis

Nitrodynamics builds **one shared engineering platform** - M1 Radio/Microwave | M2 Signal Processing | M3 Systems Engineering | M4 Embedded/Mission Software | M5 AI/ML - and amortises it across **six product verticals**: V1 AESA | V2 EW/SIGINT | V3 Counter-UAS | V4 MALE UAS | V5 AUV/USV | V6 Military AI. Effective reuse strength climbs from **25–60% in Year 1 to 97–99% by Year 10**, driving blended gross margin from 51% to **~67%** and avoiding **Rs. 994 Cr of cumulative NRE** versus a legacy-prime architecture.

## Capital Ask & Structure

- **Rs. 1,100 Cr equity** in three rounds: Y1 450 | Y2 400 | Y3 250.

- **Rs. 200 Cr venture debt** at 8%; draws Y1–Y3, retired by Y9; peak balance Rs. 200 Cr Y3–Y4.

- **Peak invested capital ÷ Y10 EBITDA ≈ 0.55x** - at the favourable end of any defense-tech comparable set.

- **Project IRR 31.9% | NPV Rs. 747 Cr @ 18% WACC.**

## Why the Y3 Peak / Y4 Inflection Holds

Four reinforcing transitions in Year 4:

1. Platform maturity crosses ~70% → vertical NRE drops materially (`Platform` rows 21–25).

2. V6 software begins attaching to every hardware line → ~200 bps blended-GM lift in a single year.

3. Wright's-Law BoM in its steepest descent (V1 AESA and V3 C-UAS cumulative-unit doublings).

4. BG collateral schedule releases from 100% → 30% across Y3–Y4, freeing ~Rs. 140–180 Cr of restricted cash back into operating cash.

## The Three Things That Can Break the Plan (ranked)

1. **Platform reuse multiplier under-performs** (`Assumptions!C26`). At 0.7x of base, Y10 GM compresses ~3–5 ppt and NPV falls to ~Rs. 510 Cr.

2. **DSO slips beyond 180 days.** At 240 DSO, peak Y3 deficit rises to ~Rs. 1,185 Cr - within the Rs. 1,300 Cr planned funding capacity, but cushion shrinks to \<Rs. 20 Cr above the Rs. 100 Cr min-cash floor. **Requires Rs. ~100 Cr standby buffer.**

3. **Pwin overstatement on MoD pursuits.** A 35% → 25% Pwin haircut on un-signed pipeline pushes Y10 backlog down by ~Rs. 3,000 Cr and the B:B taper out by ~1 year.

## The Three Things to Verify Before Term-Sheet

1. **Ind AS 38 capitalisation evidence trail** - TRL ≥ 6 substantiation per capitalised module-year (avoids restatement risk on ~Rs. 600 Cr of cumulative capitalised intangibles).

2. **BG facility commitment** from at least two banks, with the collateral tapering schedule (100→50→30→20%) in writing.

3. **180-day DSO sanity check** against MoD payment-history data on V2 ESM (the Day-1 cash engine) and at least two indigenous-development reference contracts.

## Recommendation

**Approve the Y1 Rs. 450 Cr round** with an **additional Rs. 100 Cr standby commitment** held against the 240-day-DSO downside.

Condition the Y2 round on:

- BG facility documentation from ≥ 2 banks.

- Y1 actual DSO ≤ 195 days on V2 deliveries.

- M1 / M2 module TRL evidence pack signed off by an external auditor.
